Testing Information

What To Remember Before Belt Testing

Belt testing is an important milestone. Small habits help students feel prepared, focused, and ready to do well.

  • Make sure attendance and requirements are complete.
  • Review your form, kicks, blocks, and terminology.
  • Bring the correct uniform and belt.
  • Arrive early and be ready to warm up.
  • Ask an instructor if you have questions before testing day.

Class Essentials

What Students Should Bring

A few simple habits help classes run smoothly and keep students prepared to train.

Uniform & Belt

Come dressed in the correct dobok and bring the right belt for class or testing.

Water Bottle

Stay hydrated during training, especially on longer class days or special practices.

Sparring Gear

Bring equipment on the days your class is scheduled for contact or gear work.
