Welcome To

Martial Arts Training With Heart

Ultimate Taekwondo Academy is a family-run school in Burbank focused on helping students grow stronger, more disciplined, and more confident on and off the mat. We welcome beginners, experienced students, kids, teens, and adults.

Our Instructors

Meet The Teachers

Our instructors bring years of training, leadership, and passion to every class, helping students grow in skill, confidence, and discipline.

Grand Master

Grand Master Lee

Grand Master Lee is a highly accomplished Taekwondo practitioner and instructor with 39 years of experience in teaching, coaching, refereeing, and competition. As a 7th Dan Black Belt, Korean National Champion, and International Master / Referee, he brings world-class knowledge and leadership to every student. His lifelong dedication to Taekwondo has helped shape strong athletes, disciplined students, and champions at both the state and national level.

  • 7th Dan Black Belt - WTF
  • 39 years of Taekwondo experience
  • International Master / Referee
  • Korean National Champion
  • Kyung Hee University graduate in Physical Education and Taekwondo
  • Recipient of major national and international Taekwondo honors
  • 50 students qualified for the Junior California State Team
  • 2 students became Junior National Champions
  • 1999 USA National Team Coach
